About Us

ASK Introspection combines the first letters of our names—Aayam, Sarvesh, and Kshitiz—and emphasizes the importance of introspection in mental health. By encouraging self-reflection, we aim to help individuals better understand their own mental well-being and foster personal growth.

Our Team

Aayam Bansal

Aayam believes supporting mental health is important because it addresses the rising rates of anxiety and depression, especially among young people, and promotes a healthier, more resilient community.
